Selected
Investments

Qintar Private Equity's investment portfolio encompasses a diverse array of companies, strategically selected and nurtured to realize substantial growth and make a lasting impact within their respective sectors.

From mining to construction, infrastructure development, farming, food processing, and logistics, each invested company has flourished under Qintar's guiding hand, embodying a commitment to responsible and sustainable practices.

Goldsacks Mining Ltd

Ghana, West Africa (Gold Mining)

In just two years since Qintar Private Equity's investment, Goldsacks Mining Ltd has witnessed a remarkable transformation. The infusion of capital and expertise facilitated the acquisition of state-of-the-art mining equipment, optimizing operations and significantly increasing gold output. Sustainable mining practices have enhanced the company's reputation, ensuring regulatory compliance and fostering positive community relations. Goldsacks Mining Ltd now stands as a beacon of responsible and efficient gold mining in Ghana, showcasing Qintar's commitment to ethical investments and sustainable resource management.

Mcubeh Builders Pvt Ltd

India (Infrastructure Development and Construction)

Since Qintar's investment in 2012, Mcubeh Builders Pvt Ltd has been at the forefront of India's infrastructure landscape. The strategic injection of funds propelled the company to take on ambitious projects, including urban developments and sustainable infrastructure initiatives. Their dedication to innovation and quality construction has garnered industry recognition and a growing clientele. Mcubeh Builders Pvt Ltd continues to contribute to India's infrastructural growth, reflecting Qintar's knack for identifying and supporting ventures that drive societal development.

Steel Fort Mines Pvt Ltd

Liberia (Iron Ore Mining)

Qintar Private Equity's investment in Steel Fort Mines Pvt Ltd catalyzed a boost in iron ore production within just one year. The capital injection facilitated the modernization of operations, leading to increased efficiency and a surge in ore output. The company's responsible mining practices have set a standard in the region, promoting sustainable resource extraction. Steel Fort Mines Pvt Ltd now plays a critical role in Liberia's iron ore mining industry, showcasing Qintar's ability to transform investments into thriving enterprises.

Paramount Afric Pvt Ltd

Ghana, West Africa (Large Scale Farming and Food Processing)

Over the years since Qintar's initial investment in 2007, Paramount Afric Pvt Ltd has emerged as a key player in large-scale farming and food processing in Ghana. The funds injected into the company bolstered its farming capacities and enabled the implementation of modern agricultural techniques, leading to increased crop yields. The integration of food processing facilities has further added value to their produce. Paramount Afric Pvt Ltd's success stands as a testament to Qintar's long-term commitment to fostering sustainable agriculture and ensuring food security in the region.

African World Logistics

Sierra Leone, West Africa (Haulage and Transportation)

In the span of two years following Qintar's investment, African World Logistics has revolutionized the haulage and transportation sector in Sierra Leone. The capital infusion was instrumental in expanding their fleet and adopting innovative logistics solutions. The company's reliability and efficiency in transporting goods have earned the trust of businesses across the region. African World Logistics now stands as a key player in enhancing trade and connectivity, illustrating Qintar's keen eye for investment opportunities that drive economic growth.

Medipool

Manufacturing Company of Medical Equipment

Medipool has revolutionized the medical equipment industry under Qintar's patronage. Through strategic investments, they modernized their manufacturing processes, ensuring the production of state-of-the-art medical devices. Their dedication to innovation and quality has positioned them as a trusted provider of medical equipment, contributing significantly to advancements in healthcare.

Jack Leaf

Ayurveda Hospital

Jack Leaf Ayurveda Hospital, sprawling across 20 acres, has flourished with Qintar's strategic investments. The infusion of funds facilitated the development of state-of-the-art facilities and the incorporation of advanced Ayurvedic treatments. Their commitment to holistic well-being has positioned them as a premier destination for Ayurvedic healthcare, attracting a wide range of patients seeking natural healing solutions.

Unilever C&F

(Hindustan Unilever Modern Trade)

Qintar's investment in Unilever C&F, specifically in Hindustan Unilever Modern Trade, propelled the company to new heights. With infused capital, they enhanced their distribution networks and streamlined operations, ensuring a seamless supply chain. Unilever C&F's success story is one of efficiency, increased market reach, and improved service quality.

Wadi Musaffir

General Trading Company (Import and Export from Europe)

Wadi Musaffir's success story is a testament to Qintar's expertise in investment strategies. With capital injection, the company expanded its import and export operations from Europe, diversifying its product range and market reach. Their efficient trade practices and ability to adapt to market dynamics have elevated them as a prominent player in the global trading domain.

Chickpro

Fast Food Restaurant Chain

Qintar's investment in Chickpro has catalyzed its growth into a successful fast-food restaurant chain. Through strategic funding and operational guidance, Chickpro expanded its outlets, enhanced menu offerings, and embraced technological advancements for efficient operations. The brand has now become synonymous with quality and taste, making a mark in the competitive fast-food industry.

These success stories embody Qintar Private Equity's commitment to responsible investments, strategic guidance, and fostering growth in diverse sectors, ultimately contributing to the sustainable development and advancement of each invested company.
